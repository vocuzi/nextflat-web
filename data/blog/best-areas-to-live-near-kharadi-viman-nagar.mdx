## Best Places to Live Near Pune's Kharadi and Viman Nagar

You're already headed in the right direction if you intend to relocate to Pune and are looking at the humming IT centers of **Kharadi** and **Viman Nagar**. Great infrastructure, close proximity to tech parks like EON IT Park and World Trade Center, and energetic lifestyle choices abound in both areas.

Perhaps you are, however, looking for a **nearby** location that is somewhat quieter than these fast-growing hotspots or well-connected, reasonably priced. Based on your lifestyle, budget, and preferred commute, here is a brief guide on some of the best places to live near Kharadi and Viman Nagar.

### 1. Kalyani Nagar

From both Kharadi and Viman Nagar
- **Distance**: 3–5 km
- **Vibe** Urban, sophisticated, developed

Among Pune's most upscale residential districts is Kalyani Nagar. It provides anything from quiet gated communities to tall buildings. Here you will find lots of cafés, gym buildings, stores, and even some nightlife. This could be your zone if you enjoy a city feeling combined with comfort and class.

- ✅ Great lifestyle choices; clean, green, safe
- ❌ Cons: Might be rather expensive for students or bachelors.

### 2. Wadgaon Sheri

From Kharadi and Viman Nagar
- **Distance**: 2–4km
- **Vibe**: mixed, middle range, changing crowd

Wadgaon Sheri is a sensible option, particularly if you want to strike a mix between cost and distance. Among working professionals and bachelors, it's becoming more and more popular with several new apartment buildings and co-living quarters springing up.

- ✅ Pros: plenty of rental options, reasonably priced, good connectivity.
- ❌ Cons: Still under development are some pockets.

### 3. Lohegaon

From Viman Nagar,
- **Distance**: 5 to 6 km
- **Vibe** cheap, serene, forthcoming

For people seeking a more subdued, more residential feel, Lohegaon is perfect. Although it's somewhat farther out, Nagar Road keeps it well-connected and presents fantastic rental opportunities. If you're good with a somewhat longer commute in exchange for peace and savings, this area is especially great.

- ✅ Low rent, peaceful surroundings
- ❌ Cons: Less hangouts, less nightlife

### 4. Chandan Nagar

- **Distance**: ~3 km from Kharadi
- **Vibe**: Local, cheap, expanding

Less well-known but still a pleasant neighbourhood near Kharadi and Viman Nagar is Chandan Nagar. Students and early-stage professionals looking for low rent and simple conveniences close-by will find it perfect. Thanks also to Kharadi's IT expansion, it is fast improving.

- ✅ Benefits: low rent, near to IT parks;
- ❌ Cons: Some traffic during peak hours; basic infrastructure

### 5. Mundhwa, Koregaon park annexe

-**Distance**: About 6 km from Kharadi
-**Vibe**: trendy, semi-upscale, social

Mundhwa offers mid-range rentals together with luxury homes. With access to nightlife, cafes, and boutique flats, it is starting to be the new Koregaon Park extension. If you want to enjoy the social scene of Koregaon Park but work in Kharadi, this is a fantastic alternative.

- ✅ Social scene, good connectivity
- ❌ Cons: Weekend traffic can be rather bad.

### 6. Tingre Nagar

- Distance: ~4 km from Viman Nagar
- Vibe: reasonably priced, mixed-use neighbourhood neighbourhood

Though not the most elegant choice, Tingre Nagar gives good value for money. This is worth looking at if you're looking for reasonably priced accommodation with all the conveniences—stores, clinics, and schools—all around.

- ✅ Benefits: family-friendly, low-cost houses
- ❌ Cons: Less modern homes or choices for lifestyles.

### Let's revisit

The place you decide to live in should suit your budget, way of life, and preferred travel distance. If you want something vibrant and polished, **Kalyani Nagar or Mundhwa** might be just right. **Wadgaon Sheri or Chandan Nagar** are good bets if you want to stroll to work from Kharadi. And **Lohegaon or Tingre Nagar** provide great value if your main goal is to save money while maintaining minimum comforts.

Pune has something for everyone wherever you land; and given the rate of growth, every area is just getting better.
