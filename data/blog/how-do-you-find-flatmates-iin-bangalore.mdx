## Why is it so tricky to Find Flatmates in Bangalore?

- **Noisy Facebook groups** : Most of the Flat and Flatmates Bangalore facebook groups are full of daily repetitive posts by brokers or are filled with irrelevant scams.

- **Cluttered Apps** : Apple like NoBroker and Housing exists, but are often filled by outdated posts, or posts from brokers or intermediaries.

- **Broker Scams** : Genuine flat postings by genuine flatmates end up buried deep within broker adverts and listings.

### Popular Localities for Flatmates in Bangalore

While choosing a flat to stay, always look for the proximity to your college or office, the nightlife, availability of groceries and daily need items, and social life. Here are some top areas that we recommend :

- **Koramangala** : Has loads of startups, coliving and cafe spaces.
- **Indiranagar** : A hotspot for night-life. One of the most happening places in Bangalore
- **HSR Layout** : Full with startups, has loads of greenery and residential spaces.
- **Whitefield** : Near majority tech parks, is on a little expensive side.
- **Bellandur & Sarjapur Road** : Popular for remote workers, and newbie professionals. Affordable housing as compared to rest of Bengaluru.
- **Jayanagar & J.P. Nagar** : Family-friendly neighborhoods.
